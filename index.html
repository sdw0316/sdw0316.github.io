About Me

I am a postdoctoral researcher at Korea University. My research focuses on automated software debugging by leveraging program analysis and program synthesis.

Research Interests

  • Automated program repair and overfitting patch detection
  • Program analysis and program synthesis for software debugging
  • Feedback generation and logical-error detection for programming assignments

Education

  • Integrated M.S./Ph.D. in Computer Science, Korea University (2018.03 - 2026.02). Advisor: Prof. Hakjoo Oh
  • B.S. in Computer Science, Korea University (2014.03 - 2018.02)
